The Original Bug

Human Cognition and the AI It Corrupted

Sandy B. Patterson

Before we can understand what artificial intelligence gets wrong, we have to understand what we get wrong — and why we built something so perfectly shaped to our own blind spots. The Original Bug is a rigorous and readable examination of human cognition, the systems we trust, and the machine we’ve handed our thinking to.
